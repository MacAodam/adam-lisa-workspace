# KNOWIT POWERPOINT DESIGN SPECIFICATION
**Extracted from professional Knowit presentations and brand guidelines**

## COLOR PALETTE
- **Primary Blue**: #1E3A8A (Knowit deep blue - titles, headers)  
- **Secondary Purple**: #6366F1 (Accent color for highlights)
- **Orange Accent**: #F97316 (Knowit orange - brand elements, call-outs)
- **Background**: #FFFFFF (Pure white)
- **Text Primary**: #1F2937 (Dark gray for body text)
- **Text Secondary**: #6B7280 (Medium gray for subtitles)
- **Border/Line**: #E5E7EB (Light gray for dividers)

## TYPOGRAPHY
- **Font Family**: Arial (system-wide consistency)
- **Title Font**: Arial Bold, 32-36px, #1E3A8A
- **Subtitle Font**: Arial Regular, 20-24px, #6B7280
- **Body Text**: Arial Regular, 18px, #1F2937
- **Bullet Points**: Arial Regular, 16-18px, #1F2937

## LAYOUT STRUCTURE

### Slide Header
- **Background**: White with subtle gradient accent
- **Title**: Left-aligned, Arial Bold 32px, #1E3A8A
- **Subtitle**: Left-aligned below title, Arial Regular 20px, #6B7280
- **Logo Area**: Right-aligned Knowit brand element

### Content Area  
- **Margins**: 80px left/right, 60px top/bottom
- **Line Spacing**: 1.4x for readability
- **Bullet Style**: Orange circles (●) #F97316
- **Max Bullets per Slide**: 4-5 for clarity

### Footer
- **Brand Strip**: 8px height gradient bar
- **Colors**: #1E3A8A → #6366F1 → #F97316 (left to right)
- **Page Number**: Right-aligned, Arial 12px, #6B7280

## DESIGN PRINCIPLES
1. **Minimalism**: Clean, uncluttered layout
2. **Consistency**: Same fonts, colors, spacing throughout
3. **Hierarchy**: Clear visual hierarchy with size/color
4. **Professional**: Corporate consultant-level quality
5. **Action-Oriented**: Every bullet point should be actionable
6. **Scannable**: Easy to read in 30 seconds

## CONTENT STANDARDS (CONSULTANT-LEVEL)
- **Concise**: Max 7 words per bullet point headline  
- **Specific**: Include metrics, timelines, concrete examples
- **Actionable**: Focus on "what to do" not "what is"
- **Structured**: Use frameworks (SWOT, Situation-Complication-Question-Answer)
- **Insights**: Every slide must provide value/decision support
